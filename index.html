Messi vs Ronaldo

Lionel Messi

Lionel Andrés Messi, also known as Leo Messi, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team.

Born: June 24, 1987 (age 36 years), Rosario, Argentina

Height: 1.7 m

Salary: 54 million USD (2023)

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team.

Born: February 5, 1985 (age 38 years), Hospital Dr. Nélio Mendonça, Funchal, Portugal

Height: 1.87 m

Salary: 200 million GBP (2023)

Comparison

Both Messi and Ronaldo are considered football legends and have numerous individual awards and records. The debate about who is the better player continues to be a topic of discussion among football fans.
